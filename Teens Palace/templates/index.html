About Us

Global Revival Gospel Ministries, also known as the Christian Teaching Centre, was founded in April 1987 in Nigeria by Dr. John Esuga Akpami.

Dr. Akpami is a retired medical practitioner and holds a doctorate in Pastoral Theology. He leads the Global Revival Bible Training Institute and the Christian Teaching Centre Academy, impacting lives through education, mentorship, and media outreach.
